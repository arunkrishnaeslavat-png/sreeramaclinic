At Sree Rama Clinic, we believe that mental health is just as important as physical health. Located in the heart of Vijayawada, our clinic serves as a safe haven for individuals seeking clarity, recovery, and peace of mind.

We understand that seeking help for mental health or addiction can be daunting. That is why our practice is built on three core pillars:

  • Empathy: We listen without judgment.
  • Privacy: Strict standards of patient confidentiality.
  • Science: Evidence-based medical psychiatry.
